South Eugene Cross Country Camp

August 25th-29th

9:00-11:30

Grades: 1-8

$50 per camper

Family rate:
$40 per camper

Join us for 5 days of fun running at the South Eugene track and surrounding areas! Camp activities will include group running, games, relays, drills, fun strength exercises, frolicking, yoga, and racing. Camp counselors will be members of the SEHS XC team. Professional athletes from the community will make guest appearances to speak to campers about important aspects of running such as nutrition, recovery, and even racing strategies. This camp is a great opportunity for elementary and middle school students with and without running experience to learn about the sport of running and get in some fun summer exercise! Also you get a T shirt!

  • Please register by August 20th so we have a good idea of how many campers to expect
  • Payments for camp must be made the morning of the 25th (the first day of camp). Please arrive at the South Track at 8:30 am the morning of the 25th to make camp payments. CASH OR CHECKS ONLY. Checks can be made out to South Eugene High School

This camp is a fundraiser for the South Eugene cross country program. All camp proceeds go towards sending south athletes to meets and other team expenses. Thank you for your support!
