South Eugene Cross Country Camp 2018

August 6-10th

9:00-12:00

Grades: 1-8

$80 per camper

Camp is open for registration!

Join us for 5 days of running, playing games and learning about cross country with South Eugene cross country runners! Kids will be separated into age groups, participate in age-appropriate activities and games, and will get to finish off the week with a fun cross country race on nearby soccer fields. Kids will also hear from professional athletes and coaches from the community.

Activities will include yoga, learning how to properly warm up, running technique, relays, obstacle courses, and more! Camp counselors will be current South Eugene cross country runners. This camp is a great opportunity for incoming 1st-8th graders with or without running experience to learn about the sport of running and get some fun exercise!
    Camp will be from 9:00am-12:00pm, August 6th-10th at the South Eugene Track (24th and Amazon Pkwy). Snacks and shirts will be provided. Cost is $80 per camper.

    Payments for camp must be made the morning of the 6th (the first day of camp). Please arrive at the South Track at 8:30 am the morning of the 6th to make camp payments. CASH OR CHECKS ONLY. Checks can be made out to South Eugene High School.

This camp is a fundraiser for the South Eugene cross country program. All camp proceeds go towards sending South athletes to meets and other team expenses. Thank you for your support!

If you have any questions contact us at sehsxccamp@gmail.com
